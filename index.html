About

Kallol Chakrabarti is an independent researcher specializing in governance innovation, civilizational studies, and ethical AI frameworks. Known as “Kallol,” he is the creator of the Kallol Wave Framework, recognized for contributions to governance innovation and ethical AI. His Medium article “From YouTube Learner to Policy Influencer: My Journey as Docu Helix” was featured under Recommended Stories in “Innovation Policies,” reflecting recognition by Medium’s editorial and algorithmic system.
